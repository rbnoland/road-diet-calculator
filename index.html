Methodology notes:
VSL: $12.5M (USDOT 2023), escalated at 1.07%/yr. Injury severity costs use KABCO/AIS equivalency factors per FHWA Highway Safety Manual. Travel delay = AADT × 365 × length × (1/v₂ − 1/v₁) veh-hrs/yr × blended VOT (personal × occupancy + 2× commercial rate), escalated at 1.6%/yr, discounted over the analysis period. Restriping is a one-time Year-0 capital cost (not discounted). NPV = PV(crash benefits at chosen reduction %) − restriping capital − PV(travel delay costs). Research (FHWA) typically finds road diets reduce crashes 19–47%; central estimate ~29%.
